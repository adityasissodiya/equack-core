\caption{The offline enforcement problem. While disconnected, each controller makes authorization decisions from an incomplete view of grants and revocations. After reconnection, the system deterministically agrees on which operations remain authorized, despite late-arriving revocations and conflicting decisions from independent authorities. The hard part is not granting or revoking access. The hard part is making every device agree afterward on what was authorized.}
