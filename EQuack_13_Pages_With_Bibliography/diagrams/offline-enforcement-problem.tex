\caption{The offline enforcement problem. During a partition, replicas make locally reasonable authorization decisions from different and potentially incomplete views of grants, revocations, and data operations. After reconnection, the system must merge those views and deterministically decide which operations remain authorized, despite late revocations and conflicting decisions from multiple authorities.}
\label{fig:offline-enforcement-problem}
